Birth Name: Charles Lincoln Neal III

Place of Birth: Boone Trail, North Carolina, U.S.

Date of Birth: June 1, 1978

Ethnicity: English, Scottish, German, Scots-Irish/Northern Irish

Link Neal is an American entertainer. He is one-half of the duo Rhett & Link. They are known for their YouTube series Good Mythical Morning and IFC series Commercial Kings.

A DNA test whose results Link displayed on YouTube stated that his genetic ancestry is:

*99.9% European
——–*63.9% British & Irish

Link’s paternal grandfather was Charles Lincoln Neal (the son of William Henry Neal and Myrtle Eudora Brown). William was the son of Charles Neal and Lillie Grass. Myrtle was the daughter of Burnice Burl Brown and Rena Helen Cox.

Link’s paternal grandmother was Nell Buchanan (the daughter of William Britton Buchanan and Shirley McNeill).

Link’s maternal grandfather was named Clyde F. Pate.

Link’s maternal grandmother was Mary Lucille Core (the daughter of Willie Oscar Core and Irma Ward). Mary Lucille was born in North Carolina.
