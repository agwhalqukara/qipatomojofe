With &quot;Moonlight Sunrise,&quot; TWICE Achieves Their Highest Ranking on the U.S. Spotify Chart and Tops th

Publish date: 2024-08-10

With their pre-release English single, TWICE is reaching new heights in the US! TWICE released “MOONLIGHT SUNRISE,” an English-language track from their upcoming mini album “Our Youth,” on January 20. (due out in March).

The next day, TWICE reached a new career high in the US with “MOONLIGHT SUNRISE,” which debuted at No. 39 on the U.S. Spotify Top Songs chart. The song also debuted at No. 60 on the Global Top Songs chart.

Furthermore, “MOONLIGHT SUNRISE” had already topped the iTunes Top Songs charts in at least 33 different nations as of January 22. These nations included Singapore, Japan, Brazil, and Brazil. The song became TWICE’s highest-charting single on the U.S. iTunes chart after reaching No. 2.
