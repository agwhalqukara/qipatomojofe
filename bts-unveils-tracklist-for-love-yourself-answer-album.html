BTS Unveils Tracklist for 'Love Yourself: Answer' Album

Publish date: 2024-08-27

With only a few days before they make their comeback, BTS has finally shared the tracklist for their upcoming repackaged album, Love Yourself: Answer. This album, which is the third in the Love Yourself series, includes two discs – Disc A with 16 tracks and disc B with nine.

Since it is a repackaged album, Love Yourself: Answer comes with many familiar tracks, including BTS’ singles “DNA” and “Fake Love,” as well as songs such as “Magic Shop,” “Best of Me” and “Anpanman.” It also includes remixes of “DNA” and “Fake Love,” as well as full-length editions of “Mic Drop (Steve Aoki Remix)” and “Serendipity.”

Fans would also be excited to see that this album includes Jungkook’s “Euphoria” and Jin’s “Epiphany,” which released earlier this month. Finally, Love Yourself: Answer also includes six new tracks, such as “Trivia: Just Dance,” “Trivia: Love,” “Trivia: Seesaw,” “I’m Fine,” “IDOL” and “Answer: Love Myself.” BTS has not distinguished any of these songs as being their next single.

The group teased some of the themes on this album with the release of their concept photos and “notes” series, which explains some events in the group’s long-running narrative.

Love Yourself: Answer will be released on August 24th. Unlike previous comebacks, the group will be debuting the songs live through their upcoming tour, rather than through television performances. BTS’ tour is named after their Love Yourself series and will see the septet travel to different parts of Asia, North America and Europe. Their final tour stop will be in Queens, New York at Citi Field stadium. It will be the group’s first U.S. stadium show and and also the first time a K-pop group will ever perform there. Tickets for the show went on sale on Friday, August 17th and sold out within minutes.
