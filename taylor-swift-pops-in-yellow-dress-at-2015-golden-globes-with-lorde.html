Taylor Swift Pops in Yellow Dress at 2015 Golden Globes With Lorde

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Hello, sunshine!

Taylor Swift may not have been a nominee at the 2015 Golden Globes in Beverly Hills on Sunday, Jan. 11, but that didn’t stop her from slaying the red carpet at the afterparties!

The “Blank Space” singer, 25, donned a strapless Jenny Packham chiffon dress in a delicious shade of marigold. Swift also added a molten clutch by Jimmy Choo, bold cluster earrings by Lorraine Schwartz (each one boasting yellow and white diamonds), and kite-shaped rings.

On the beauty front, Swift literally shined thanks to the golden shimmer on her eyelids, which she paired with her signature red statement lip. Her wavy blonde tresses were styled in a loose, romantic updo.

And she wasn’t the only lady in yellow: Naomi Watts, Jenna Dewan Tatum, and Leslie Mann all modeled the happy hue, creating a red carpet trend.

But the Globes was more than an occasion for the Grammy winner to go glam: They turned into quite the (famous) girls’ night out! On the red carpet and inside HBO’s Official Golden Globe Awards Afterparty, Swift was spotted embracing two of her besties, nominee Lorde (in a Narciso Rodriguez suit and crop top combo) and Selena Gomez (in a white Kaufmanfranco dress and Jimmy Choo heels).

The “Style” chanteuse also caught up with Girls creator and actress Lena Dunham, who was celebrating her nomination and the fourth season premiere of her hit series that night. Dunham even planted a smooch on Swift’s cheek at one point, before the singer had to hop on to the next affair.
