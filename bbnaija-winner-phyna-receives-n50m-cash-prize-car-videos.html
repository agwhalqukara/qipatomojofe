BBNaija Winner, Phyna Receives N50m Cash Prize, Car (VIDEOS)

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Josephina “Phyna” Otabor, the w inner of Big Brother Naija (BBNaija) 2022, has gotten her N50 million check.

Phyna, 25, was announced winner of the Big Brother Naija Level Up edition during the finale on Sunday, October 2, 2022.

Phyna received the cheque and the automobile on Tuesday, October 4, as part of the grand reward for taking first place in the reality TV contest.

As part of the prize package, Phyna also receives a cheque for N50 million, a Pepsi supply, a bitcoin from Quidax, soap goods from Unik, electronics from Nexus, dairy products from Dano, MunchIT packs, and cartons of Waw detergent.

She is also eligible for further prizes, including a Tecno Camon 19, an Innoson G5T from Innoson Vehicles, and an all-expenses-paid trip for two to Dubai from Travelbeta.

Prior to accepting the payment, Phyna spoke with the media and answered questions regarding her 72-day appearance on the show.
