Sarah Jessica Parker Rocks Swimsuit at Beach With Matthew Broderick

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Toned, tan, and totally loving summer! Sarah Jessica Parker rocked a one-piece swimsuit on Sunday, Aug. 17, while happily frolicking on the beach in the Hamptons with husband Matthew Broderick and their daughter Marion.

Parker, 49, showed off her enviable figure in the slimming navy suit which revealed the star’s long and muscular legs. The Sex and the City actress joyfully threw up her arms at one point after dipping her toes in the Atlantic Ocean.

Broderick, 52, joined his wife for the outdoor excursion in floral swim trunks and a striped T-shirt. The married couple of 17 years were snapped holding beach toys as they helped their 5-year-old daughter build sandcastles. See more beach pics of SJP here.

The family was in Montauk after the weekend wedding of Parker’s “dear friend” Melinda Relyea. Parker, who served as a bridesmaid in the ceremony held in Connecticut on Saturday, shared several Instagram photos from the eventful weekend.
